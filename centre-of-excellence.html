Centre of Excellence

Centre of Excellence (CoE)

To promote and enhance skill development and employment generation activities for youth, MSPL is working under various schemes to impart skills in various trades & locations. MSPL provides the youth with industry relevant skills & On the Job Training required for making them employable.

Skill Development is a powerful tool to empower the people and improves their social acceptance. It is complemented by a good economic growth and enhances employability. India is towards the verge of being a developed country with needs a greater number of skilled and knowledgeable people.


Flexible, modular framework to enable youth to obtain industry relevant qualifications

  • To provide the students with a flexible, modular system for pursuing their higher education from 10th Pass to ITI/Diploma/Degree Level while they work.
  • To recognize prior learning & on the job learning and translated into credits which can be accumulated and will form a part of the earned qualification
